Foundation Flames

Founded in 1922, Werner Company is an American manufacturer specializing in ladders, climbing equipment, and various job site and fall protection products. With its headquarters in Itasca, Illinois, Werner has grown to become a leading brand recognized for its innovation, safety, and reliability.

Safety

At Werner Ladder Company, safety is our top priority. We meticulously engineer our products to exceed industry standards, ensuring maximum stability and reliability. Through rigorous testing and continuous innovation, we strive to create the safest climbing solutions available. Trust Werner for unparalleled safety in every climb.

Quality Product

Werner products are built to meet or exceed all applicable American National Standards Institute (ANSI) and Occupational Safety and Health Administration (OSHA) code requirements. Indeed, supplying the safest equipment possible is our top priority.
